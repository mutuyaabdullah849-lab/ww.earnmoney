❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum saving amount?

The minimum saving amount is 5,000 UGX. You can save up to 1,000,000 UGX per transaction.

How long does it take to confirm my savings?

Your savings are confirmed within 24 hours after submission and verification by our treasurer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel), Bank Transfers, Cash payments, and Online Transfers.

Can I withdraw my savings anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw your savings after completing 3 months of membership. Some conditions may apply.

How do I qualify for a loan?

You must be a member for at least 3 months with minimum savings of 50,000 UGX. Maximum loan: 5,000,000 UGX. Approval is based on savings and group guarantee.

What is the loan approval process?

1. Submit application online or to treasurer. 2. Committee review (24-48 hours). 3. Approval and fund disbursement. 4. Set repayment schedule.

What are the interest rates?

Interest on savings: 5% per annum (quarterly). Loan rates: Quick Loan 8%, Standard Loan 10%, Business Loan 12% per annum.

Can I get a business loan?

Yes! Our Business Loans range from 2,000,000 to 5,000,000 UGX with 12-24 month repayment terms. Submit a brief business plan.

How can I join the group?

Contact any group official. You need to register, pay a one-time registration fee of 10,000 UGX, and make your first savings deposit.

What if I can't repay my loan on time?

Contact the treasurer immediately. We offer flexible rescheduling options to help you manage your repayment.
